Historical Places in Saudi Arabia

1. Diriyah

Diriyah is a UNESCO World Heritage site, known for its mud-brick architecture and historical significance as the original home of the Saudi royal family.

2. Al-Ula

Al-Ula is famous for its stunning rock formations and ancient tombs, including the UNESCO-listed Madain Saleh.
